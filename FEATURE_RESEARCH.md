## Platform Overview

| Platform | Primary Focus | Target Audience | Founded |
|----------|--------------|-----------------|---------|
| **Linktree** | Link-in-bio hub | Creators, influencers, brands, small businesses | 2016 |
| **Lnk.Bio** | Lightweight link-in-bio | Creators, social media users, budget-conscious users | 2018 |
| **Carrd** | One-page website builder | Freelancers, small businesses, personal sites, portfolios | 2016 |
| **Beacons** | All-in-one creator platform | Content creators, influencers, digital entrepreneurs | 2020 |

### Positioning

- **Linktree** is the market leader and most recognized link-in-bio tool. It focuses on being a simple, polished hub for all your links with strong analytics and commerce bolt-ons.
- **Lnk.Bio** is a budget-friendly alternative with a unique one-time payment option. It emphasizes simplicity, unlimited links on the free tier, and flexible layouts.
- **Carrd** is not a traditional link-in-bio tool — it's a full one-page website builder. It offers far more design control but requires more effort to set up. Ideal for landing pages, portfolios, and mini-sites.
- **Beacons** is the most feature-rich, positioning itself as an all-in-one creator business platform with built-in email marketing, a digital store, AI tools, media kits, and invoicing.

## Unique / Differentiating Features

### Linktree
- **Linktree Shops**: Curate products from 150+ major retailers and earn affiliate commission — essentially a built-in affiliate storefront.
- **NFT-gated links**: Lock links behind NFT ownership verification using a smart contract address.
- **Instagram DM automation**: Automatically send DMs to drive traffic to your Linktree.
- **Sensitive content warnings**: Add content warnings before users access certain links.
- **Admin roles** (Pro+): Grant team members access to manage your Linktree.
- **Affiliate programs**: Direct integrations with Commission Junction, Apple Performance Partners, and Amazon Associates.
- **Widest payment support**: PayPal, Square, and Venmo for tips and donations.

### Lnk.Bio
- **One-time payment model**: Pay once ($9.99 or $24.99) and use forever — no recurring fees.
- **Instagram/TikTok grid mirror**: Display your IG or TikTok feed as a visual grid with custom links per post.
- **34 embed services**: Broadest range of embeddable media sources.
- **28,179 fonts**: Massive font library for typographic customization.
- **24 layout options**: Choose from list, grid, IG mirror, and many more layout styles.
- **Built-in IG/TikTok scheduler**: Schedule social posts from the same dashboard.
- **HubSpot form embedding**: Directly embed HubSpot forms for lead generation.
- **Developer access / API**: Free plan includes API access for developers.

### Carrd
- **Full page builder**: Not a link list — a complete one-page website builder with drag-and-drop elements.
- **Custom code** (Pro Standard+): Inject arbitrary HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for full design control.
- **Advanced form integrations** (Pro Plus): Connect forms to Zapier, Make, n8n, and Airtable for complex workflows.
- **Site downloads** (Pro Plus): Download your complete site as unminified HTML, CSS, and JS files.
- **Password protection** (Pro Plus): Gate entire sites behind a password.
- **Dynamic variables** (Pro Plus): Use URL parameters and variables to dynamically change page content.
- **Multiple sites**: Manage up to 100 sites (Pro Max) from a single account.
- **Incredibly affordable**: Full-featured site building for as little as $9/year.
- **99.98% uptime**: Enterprise-grade reliability.

### Beacons
- **All-in-one creator platform**: Link-in-bio, store, email marketing, media kits, invoicing, AI tools — all integrated.
- **AI content engine**: Generate emails, product descriptions, images, bios, and outreach — with an AI that learns your style.
- **Built-in media kit**: Auto-generated, always-updated media kit with your social stats for brand deals.
- **Native invoicing**: Create and send invoices directly from the platform.
- **Course creation**: Build and sell online courses with video hosting (Store Pro+).
- **Membership / subscription support**: Sell recurring access to exclusive content.
- **Appointment scheduling**: Book coaching calls or consultations with multi-calendar sync.
- **Buy now, pay later**: Offer installment payments to customers.
- **Physical NFC card** (Business Pro): A branded card that links to your Beacons page when tapped.
- **Google Workspace integration** (Business Pro): Professional email and productivity tools.
- **Multi-currency support**: Accept payments in multiple currencies.
- **Geo-targeted campaigns** (Creator Pro+): Send different email content based on subscriber location.
